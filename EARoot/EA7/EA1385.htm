A HumanResourceTypeUsage that is a type of subOrganisation which asserts that a state of one type of Organisation, the OrganisationType, is typically the parent of another, the OrganisationTypeUsage. In other words, one type of Organisation is typically the parent of another for a period of time. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>E.g. a squadron may be part of a battalion<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Note: was called "SubOrganisation" in M3.<br /></p>
